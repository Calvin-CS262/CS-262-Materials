Past Rides:
You can use this section of the Rides page to look at past rides that you have been on.

Imagine that you want to remember when you carpooled to a certain destination. To do this you can:
1. Click on the Rides page button at the top right of the home page.
2. Scroll down to the Past Rides section.
3. Look for the ride you are searching for.
